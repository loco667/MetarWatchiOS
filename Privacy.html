Privacy Policy Effective date: 26.03.2026 MetarWatch iOS (“the App”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what data the App processes, why, and how it is handled. 1. Data We Process The App may process: - Location data (when you grant permission): used to find the nearest airport. - Airport identifiers you enter or select (for example ICAO/IATA codes). - Weather data fetched for airports (METAR/TAF text and related timestamps). - App preferences (for example selected/starred widget airport slots). 2. How Data Is Used We use data only to provide core app features: - Show METAR/TAF for selected airports. - Find the nearest airport with available METAR data. - Populate watch and lock-screen widgets. - Sync monitored airport list between iPhone and Apple Watch. 3. Data Storage App data is stored locally on your device using Apple-provided local storage (App Group/UserDefaults), including: - Saved airport list - Selected widget airport(s) - Cached METAR/TAF snapshots - Last known location used for nearest-airport lookup 4. Network Requests To retrieve aviation weather, the App sends requests to: - https://aviationweather.gov/api/data/ These requests may include: - Your IP address - Airport identifiers (for METAR/TAF lookups) - A geographic bounding box derived from your location (for nearest-station search) We do not operate that external service and are not responsible for its independent privacy practices. 5. Data Sharing We do not sell personal data. We do not share your data with data brokers or advertisers. 6. Analytics, Tracking, and Advertising The App does not use third-party analytics SDKs, ad SDKs, or cross-app tracking. 7. Your Choices - You can deny or revoke location access at any time in iOS/watchOS Settings. - You can remove saved airports inside the App. - You can delete the App to remove locally stored app data from your device. 8. Children’s Privacy The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children. 9. Changes to This Policy We may update this policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted with a new effective date. 10. Contact If you have questions about this policy, contact: metarwatchios@plek2.aero
